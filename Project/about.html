Dogs and cats can be viewed or added on the pets page.

Dogs and cats remaine in the data base for all time, but are not referanced if they are not currently in house.

Dogs and cats can be in house or not by adding and deleting guest entries.

Upon adding a guest entry you can select the dog or cat that the stay is for.

Upon adding a guest entry you can select package the guest is assigned.

Upon selecting a package the relatedd activites are also added to the activities section but have no employees assigned.

Employees can be added or removed from the employees page.

Employees can be assigned to activities from activities page.
